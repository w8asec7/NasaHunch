Depth Dive

Our Product

Our product ensures astronaut safety by determining whether a surface is safe to walk on. It utilizes a LIDAR sensor and an Arduino system powered by Lumen AI, which analyzes the environment in real time to assess terrain stability. Additionally, our system can store and analyze LIDAR sensor data for further evaluation and research.

Our Mission

Future Plans

In the future, we plan to develop an advanced AI that will process LIDAR sensor data to generate a detailed 3D environment in Unity. This will enable developers to create realistic simulators for the Moon and other space environments.
